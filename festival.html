Salzburg Festival

Salzburg Festival is an annual music and drama festival held in Salzburg Austria. It is one of the most prestigious and renowned festival in the world, attracting artists and audiences from around the globe. The festival typically takes place during the summer months, from late July to August, and features a diverse program of opera, classical music concerts, theater performances, and other cultural events. Salzburg Festival is known for its high artistic standards and has a rich history dating back to its founding in 1920. The Festival is held in various venues throughout the city, including the historic Salzburg Cathedral and the open-art theater on the Monchsberg hill.

Spring Festival (Chinese New Years)

Chinese New Years, also known as the Spring Festival,is the most important traditional festival in china. It marks the beginning of the lunar new year and is celebrated with various customs and traditional activities. The Festival usually lasts for 15days, stsrting from the first day of the lunar calender. Common traditional activities during the Spring Festival include family reunions, feasting on special foods, giving red envelopes containing money for good luck, and various cultural performances such as dragon and lion dances. The Spring Festival is a time for families to come together, honor ancestors, and wish for prosperity and good fortune in the coming year.
